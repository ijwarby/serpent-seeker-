Snakes Around The Globe

Snakes, remarkable creatures found across every continent except Antarctica, Iceland, Ireland, Greenland, and New Zealand, have demonstrated astonishing adaptability to diverse habitats. With over 3000 species inhabiting our planet, their presence spans from arid deserts to lush rainforests, and even within oceanic realms.

Welcome to Serpent Seeker, a platform dedicated to igniting the curiosity of snake enthusiasts worldwide. Here, you'll embark on a journey to discover these fascinating reptiles and their global distribution. Explore populated continents, delve into their habitats, and test your knowledge with engaging quizzes. Join us in unraveling the mysteries of the world!
