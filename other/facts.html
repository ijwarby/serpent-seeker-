Cobra Facts

  • Venomous Predators: Cobras are renowned for their venomous bites.
  • Iconic Hood: They're instantly recognizable by the hood they flare when threatened.
  • Global Presence: Found across Africa and Asia, they thrive in diverse habitats.

Breaking Records

  • The longest venomous snake, the King Cobra, can reach lengths up to 18 feet.
  • The most venomous snake, the Inland Taipan, possesses a deadly neurotoxic venom.
  • The fiercest snake, the Black Mamba, is known for its speed and aggression.

Snake Diets

  • Diverse Diets: Cobras consume a variety of prey, including rodents, birds, and even other snakes.
  • Egg Eaters: Some cobras, like the King Cobra, have a penchant for raiding bird nests for eggs.
  • Top Predators: Cobras play a crucial role in controlling rodent populations, helping to maintain ecosystem balance.
